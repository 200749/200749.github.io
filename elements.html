Research Questions

These questions are answered, and cited throughout this page.

What are some interesting different types of instruments that I could build?

Kalimba

If I made a kalimba what are some advantages, and disadvantages?

  • Advantage- It would be completely homemade, and I would be able to find the materials easily around my house
  • Disadvantage- It would be too easy to build, and too easy to find materials. It also seems like the instructions are very vague, and not very well instructed.
  • I could build it using materials like tin cans, and cutting a hole in the middle, and possibly bending wire (or metal) in order to create the different parts for the finger piano)

Citation with the text quote

DeathSuperMario. "How I Made Kalimba." Instructables.com. Autodesk, 2015. Web. 03 Mar. 2016.

"First someone in family drunk tea. There I got tin can. Then I cut aluminum rods and one bigger rod.I drilled holes on can, rods and bolted rods on can."

Ukelule

If I made a ukelele what are some advantages, and disadvantages?

  • Advantage- It would be challenging to make the sides of the ukelele, and it wouldn’t be totally difficult.
  • Disadvantage- It would be the same as everyone else, and wouldn’t look professional. i also wouldn’t know where to source the materials, in order to build the ukelele.

    • Where would I get the strings?

  • I could source them, but i would still need to pay for them.

    • Where would I get the wood?

  • Some of the supermarkets give the wood, as they only need it to transport the goods, and don’t need it after.

Citation with the text quote

Matthias Wandel. "Building a Ukulele." Building a Ukulele. Woodgears, 6 Apr. 2013. Web. 05 Mar. 2016.

"Having an experienced guitar builder such as Pat to turn to for advice gave me the courage to build one. But once I got started, I experimented with how I could build it without the specialized equipment that many guitar builders use. I will also be covering Pat Hawley's more professional build of a ukulele alongside my experiments. I also have Free ukulele plans available "
